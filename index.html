Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Purpose-built for the way money actually works here.

Dual currency

Enter expenses in LBP or USD. Everything's stored in lira and shown in both, with an editable exchange rate.

Monthly view

Income, expenses and savings at a glance, with a clean table and a per-category doughnut chart.

Yearly reports

See every month side by side, expand any month for a category breakdown, and watch the trend on a bar chart.

Add via WhatsApp

Text "Toters 45k groceries" to your number and it's parsed, converted and logged — with an instant reply.

Smart categories

21 colour-coded categories — from Groceries to $ Exchange — so your spending sorts itself visually.

Light & dark

A warm, calm interface that follows your taste — switch themes with a single tap, on any device.
